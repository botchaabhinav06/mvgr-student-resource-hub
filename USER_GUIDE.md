# User Guide - MVGR Student Resource Hub

This guide provides an overview for navigating and using the platform based on your role.

## For Students

1. **Login**: Use your college-issued credentials to access the hub.
2. **Current Semester Materials**: View and preview study materials curated for your current semester. Access is automatically filtered to your department, year, and semester.
3. **All Materials**: Browse all available materials within your department across all years and semesters.
4. **Question Papers**: Access repository of past question papers filtered by your department.
5. **Profile**: View your academic details. You can update your **Year** and **Semester**. Your **Department** is locked.
6. **Preview/Download**: Click on a resource to preview it. Use the provided tools to download the file directly.

## For Faculty

1. **Login**: Use your credentials.
2. **Upload Material**: Click "Upload Material". Fill in file details (Title, Course Code, Dept, Year, Sem). Upload the PDF.
3. **Manage Materials**: Browse your uploaded materials. You can preview, download, or delete them.
4. **Filters**: Use the sidebar and filter tools to manage your specific subject materials.

## For Administrators

1. **Login**: Access the administrator dashboard.
2. **User Management**: Open "User Management" to view and search for all users.
3. **Edit Profile**: You can safely edit a student's **Department**, **Year**, and **Semester**, with system-wide normalization applied automatically.
4. **Normalization Tools**: Run diagnostics and normalization updates when needed to keep data integrity.
5. **Safety**: All edits are audited. Proceed with caution when editing student academic fields.
